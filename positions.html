Open Positions

ADML embraces the best talents for exciting projects. Please read this page carefully for opening position application process details and policies, as well as available positions to-date. We greatly appreciate your interest and look forward to hearing from you!

What will you do?

Graduate students at ADML work on cutting-edge topics in multidisciplinary research field, and receive significant training on lattice and free-form design/optimization, various AM technologies, eco-design and sustainable product development skills, manufacturing process modeling and optimization, dimensional metrology, bio-compatible materials and their applications in AM field. The students also have close collaborations with bio-engineering and materials engineering professors as well as Québec’s aerospace industry.

Application Process

ADML actively seeks self-motivated graduate students, postdocs, and visiting students/scholars for a wide range of research projects aforementioned above. Undergraduate students for thesis or summer research are also welcomed. To apply for the open positions, please contact Prof.Zhao by email. Please provide the following information about your candidacy, and note that inquiries without any of these components will NOT be answered.

  1. Specify the area(s) and the position (undergraduate, master, Ph.D., or postdoc) you are interested in;
  2. Briefly summarize your previous research experience relevant to the position you are applying for;
  3. Indicate any external financial support/scholarship you will hold;
  4. Attach a detailed CV, including undergraduate and graduate GPAs, research experience, expertise, and a list of publications.

Every year, mass email inquiries are received. Please understand ONLY applicants with potential fits with our positions will be contacted. Projects currently open are detailed below, as well as information regarding a special joint scholar program between McGill and China.

Opportunities

For Ph.D. and postdoc applicants from China, McGill offer a joint scholarship program with Chinese Scholarship Council (CSC). Once admitted through the program, McGill will cover tuition fees of the students/postdoc during their studies/research. We welcome CSC applicants, and feel free to contact Prof. Zhao for questions regarding application.
